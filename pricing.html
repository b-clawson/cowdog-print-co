Digitizing converts your artwork into a machine stitch file — charged once per design. Reorders and future orders using the same design are never charged again. When multiple digitizing fees apply, only the highest fee is charged.

Example 1 — 100 hats, front logo: Embroidery 100 × $8.00 = $800 · Digitizing $30 · Total $830 + blanks

Example 2 — 100 hats, front + back + left side (3 placements): Front 100 × $8.00 = $800 · Back 100 × $6.40 = $640 (−20%) · Left Side 100 × $6.40 = $640 (−20%) · Digitizing $30 · Total $2,110 + blanks ($21.10/pc)

Example 3 — 50 polos, left chest: Embroidery 50 × $6.00 = $300 · Digitizing $30 · Total $330 + blanks

Example 4 — 100 hoodies, full front + full back: Full Front 100 × $9.00 = $900 · Full Back 100 × $12.80 = $1,280 (−20%) · Digitizing $85 (highest fee) · Total $2,265 + blanks ($22.65/pc)
