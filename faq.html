Screen Printing

Water-based inks soak into the fabric for a soft, breathable hand feel. Plastisol sits on top and feels thicker. We only use water-based inks — they look better, feel better, and age beautifully. No shortcuts.
Up to 6 colors per location. Each color is a separate screen. Dark garments require an additional white underbase (counts as +1 color).
Standard: Up to 15"×18" — great for most designs.
Jumbo: Up to 17"×23" — covers nearly the entire front or back. Perfect for oversized graphics and poster-style prints. Most shops cap at 15"×18", so we're one of the few in the Midwest offering true jumbo prints at production scale.
We can source garments for you (most common) or print on garments you provide. If you're supplying, make sure they're 100% cotton or cotton-blend and pre-washed. Send us details before ordering so we can confirm they'll work for water-based printing.

Embroidery

Digitizing converts your artwork into a stitch file for embroidery. It's a one-time fee per design:
  • Hat / Left Chest: $30
  • Full Front: $85
  • Full Back: $150
Once digitized, reorders and future orders using that design are never charged again.
Hat / Left Chest: Up to 8,000 stitches
Full Front: 8,000–15,000 stitches
Full Back: 15,000–30,000 stitches

Most logos fall well within these limits. If your design is larger, we'll let you know and provide a custom quote.
Embroidery works best on sturdy fabrics like cotton twill (hats), polos, fleece, and canvas. Lightweight or stretchy fabrics (like jersey tees) can be tricky. If you're unsure, let us know what you're working with — we'll advise on what's possible.
