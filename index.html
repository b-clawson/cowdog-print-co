What we do.

OptionsStandard (15"×18") · Jumbo (17"×23")
Inks100% water-based only
Suggested Min.48+ pieces
Turnaround2–3 weeks
Our bread and butter. We print on tees, sweatshirts, tote bags, posters, and more using 100% water-based inks — no plastisol, no shortcuts. Up to 6 colors per location, soft hand feel, prints that age beautifully. Standard and jumbo sizing available depending on how big you want to go. While we suggest 48+ pieces for the best pricing, we don't enforce strict minimums — we want to support creatives and small, indie businesses having the merch they've always dreamed of.
IncludesNeck tags · Fold & poly · Hang tags · Fulfillment
Lead Time+3 days to standard turnaround
Running a label, restocking for a tour, or building inventory for a shop? We handle the full back end — printed neck tags, folding and bagging, hang tag application, and fulfillment. Everything is à la carte so you only pay for what you actually need. We keep quality consistent across runs so you're never scrambling before a launch.
Good forFirst-time merch · New label launches · Rebrands
Don't just print anything — make something people actually want to wear. We help you think through art direction, colorways, garment selection, and what your audience will actually buy. Whether you're starting from a rough sketch or a finished file, we'll make sure it's set up right before it ever hits a screen.
Good forLimited drops · Independent artists · Musicians · Makers
Some of our favorite work happens when we team up directly with artists on limited runs. Bring your designs — we'll handle the rest and make it look incredible. We've worked with illustrators, musicians, and independent brands who want merch that actually reflects their work, not something generic off a template.
