## 3. Upload Photos (Critical!)

### Required Photos:
1. **Logo** - `assets/logo-white.svg` or PNG version
2. **Cover photo** - `assets/showroom.jpg` (optimized)
3. **Interior** - Workshop/press photos (5-10 photos)
4. **Exterior** - Building storefront
5. **Products** - Recent prints (10+ photos)
6. **Team** - Staff working (if comfortable)

**Photo Tips:**
- High resolution (720px minimum)
- Good lighting
- Show your work process
- Upload 20-30 photos total
